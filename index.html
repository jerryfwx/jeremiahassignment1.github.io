ABOUT US

Discover a world of benefits in Food & Entertainment, Fitness & Adventure and Family & Enrichment at the newly refurbished SAFRA club in Toa Payoh. After a $52 million revamp completed in 2013, the club now boasts of a 24,000 sq m floor space that is three times larger than its original facility. We’re the go-to destination for all social, recreational, sports and educational facilities for NSmen and their families. Formed in 1972, SAFRA’s goal was to aid the Singapore Armed Forces (SAF) enhance camaraderie and boost morale among National Servicemen. Today, there is a network of clubs located at Jurong, Mount Faber, Punggol, Tampines, Toa Payoh and Yishun. In addition to clubs, we offer a host of activities, value-added services and membership perks that meet the lifestyle needs of our community of more than 430,000 SAFRA members. Join us today!
